Parametric Nonlinear System Identification

Sloppiness, Information Gain, and Practical Strategies

Register Now

Abstract

This full-day workshop focuses on parametric nonlinear system identification, highlighting sloppiness, identifiability, and information-theoretic experiment design. Participants will explore geometric perspectives and β-information gain to improve model understanding and control.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand sloppiness as a geometric property
  • Analyze sensitivity spectra
  • Apply β-information gain
  • Improve prediction and control strategies
